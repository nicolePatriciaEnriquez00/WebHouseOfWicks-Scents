About

Behind The Brand

Welcome to House of Wicks & Scents, your haven for exquisite scented candles and captivating eau de parfums.

We are a family-owned business dedicated to creating exceptional fragrances that bring joy, warmth, and a touch of luxury to your everyday moments.

Why House of Wicks & Scents

We understand the transformative power of scent. It has the ability to uplift our spirits, evoke cherished memories, and create a comforting atmosphere in our homes.

With this passion in our hearts, we extend our expertise beyond candles to also offer a range of alluring eau de parfums, ensuring that you can carry your favorite scents with you wherever you go.

Amazing Products Made With Passion

Our scented candles are meticulously hand-poured using natural, sustainable ingredients. We carefully select premium waxes and blend them with thoughtfully crafted fragrance oils to create candles that burn cleanly and evenly, filling your space with enchanting aromas.

Each candle is lovingly crafted with attention to detail, resulting in a beautiful and elegant addition to your home décor.

Our Dedication And Commitment

Our commitment to quality extends to our eau de parfums as well. We collaborate with skilled perfumers who possess a deep understanding of scent composition and the artistry of fragrance creation.

Using the finest ingredients sourced from around the world, we craft perfumes that capture the essence of sophistication, individuality, and timeless elegance. Our fragrances evolve gracefully, revealing layers of complexity and inviting others to be captivated by your unique presence.

An Eco-Conscious Team

We also believe in sustainability and ethical practices. We strive to minimize our environmental footprint by using recyclable materials for our packaging and supporting responsible sourcing initiatives.

We take pride in our small-batch production, ensuring that each candle and perfume is carefully crafted with the utmost care and attention to detail.

Our Purpose

As a family-owned business, we value the personal connection with our customers. We are here to guide and inspire you in your journey of discovering the perfect fragrance that resonates with your desires and personality. We invite you to explore our collection of scented candles and eau de parfums, and let us illuminate your world with our captivating fragrances.

"At House of Wicks & Scents, we believe that fragrance has the remarkable ability to weave stories, create ambiance, and evoke emotions. With our scented candles and eau de parfums, we invite you to immerse yourself in a symphony of scents that will ignite your senses and leave an indelible mark on your soul."
